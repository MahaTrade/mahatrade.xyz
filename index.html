MahaTrade

MahaTrade is a London-based trading firm backed by MahaDAO.com that focuses specifically on market-making, quant strategies and arbitrage. We operate across decentralised (layer 1 & layer 2) and centralised cryptocurrency markets.


Our focus is mostly on stablecoins, mostly on ARTH and USD-peged stablecoins. But we also help create efficient and liquid markets for other mid & low cap projects.
