MahaTrade

MahaTrade is a London-based trading/market-making firm by MahaDAO.com that focuses specifically on market-making, quant strategies and arbitrage. We operate across decentralised (layer 1 & layer 2) and centralised cryptocurrency markets.


Our expertise has been on stablecoins, mostly on the ARTH stablecoin. But we also help create efficient and liquid markets for other mid & low cap stablecoins.
